The Highlands
of Sephu

Located in the Wangdue Phodrang district of central Bhutan, the Sephu region is a high-altitude pastoral landscape where yak herding communities have maintained seasonal migration routes for generations. Surrounded by dense forests, glacial rivers, and sacred mountain peaks, Sephu's traditions reflect a deep ecological and cosmological relationship with the land. This archive features a film, a photo essay, and an ethnographic vignette from fieldwork in the region.
