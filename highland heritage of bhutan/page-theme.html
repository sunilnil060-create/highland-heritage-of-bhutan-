The Living
Highland Ecology

Bhutan's highlands are among the most ecologically intact mountain environments on Earth. From the alpine meadows of Haa and the sacred watershed of Tshonapata in Sephu, to the forested ridgelines above Thimphu, these landscapes are not wilderness in the western sense — they are managed, storied, and inhabited spaces. This archive gathers environmental perspectives across three media forms, documenting how highland communities read, use, and protect their natural world.
