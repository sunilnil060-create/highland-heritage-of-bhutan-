Sacred Culture
of Thimphu

Thimphu, Bhutan's capital, sits at the intersection of highland tradition and modern Bhutanese identity. Its dzongs, sacred sites, and living ritual practices make it a vital center of cultural preservation. This archive documents the mask dances, oral traditions, and spiritual geographies that define Thimphu's highland heritage — resources created in partnership with RTC faculty and local community leaders.
