The Highlands
of Haa

Nestled in western Bhutan along the border with Tibet, the Haa Valley is one of the kingdom's most secluded and spiritually significant highland regions. Known for its cliffside temples, ancient pastoral routes, and sacred lakes, Haa is home to communities whose traditions and cosmologies are deeply woven into the mountain landscape. This archive features a film, a photo essay, and an ethnographic vignette from fieldwork in the region.
